Our story

Nalca Biotech was born in 2023 when Tomás Niklitschek and Felipe Tapia met in Chile after each of them had returned from studying and working abroad in Europe. Tom Vaughan joined soon afterwards at the start of 2024 to kickstart product development. Our first customer installation in Santiago came less than six months later.

Our values

Our strength lies in our diversity. Our work is made better by a wide variety of opinions and perspectives. We also understand the threat global warming poses and strive to mitigate its impact through the work we do.
