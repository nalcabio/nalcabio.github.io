Continuous fermentation in the lab

Nalca 1

The Nalca 1 can be easily integrated with shake flasks or benchtop bioreactors, and is compatible with a wide variety of biological products, like bacteria, yeast, and peptides, that use a suspension culture and are genetically stable over time. It's perfect for product development or small production runs. Powered by our nalcaOS, it can be easily operated by anyone via its web-based dashboard.
