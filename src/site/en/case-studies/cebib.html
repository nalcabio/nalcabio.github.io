Early validation of recombinant protein expression required either low-control flask experiments or costly, time-intensive bioreactor runs, limiting speed and experimental throughput.

The Nalca Biotech Solution: Feasibility & Validation ðŸ”¥

Stage 1: Focus on biomass growth; Stage 2: Focus on production of the target product. A Nalca 1 was used as an automated, continuous-simplified system to evaluate expression performance between flask scale and fed-batch reactors, without the cost or complexity of operating a bioreactor.

Outcomes

  • Fast. Faster evaluation of expression stability and construct performance.
  • Cost-effective. Reduced reliance on bioreactor time and associated operating costs.
  • Prepared to scale. Enabled early de-risking before advancing to fed-batch and larger-scale runs.
