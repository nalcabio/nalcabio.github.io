Feasability Test

Before moving into continuous bioprocessing there are three main questions that need to be answered: 1) Do you have a suspension-growing cell? 1) Is the productivity of the system stable over time? 2) Is the system genetically stable over time? Answering these questions can reduce the risks of failure of your project. All you need is a suspension growing cell and shake flasks. You don't need to invest in expensive continuous bioreactors to answer these questions. We have the knowledge to set a simplified-continuous process with all the elements that you already have in your lab. Send us an email and we will share with you a demo.
